Welcome to E.T.M.O

In E.T.M.O (Extract, Tax, and Money Operation), you’re not just a player — you’re the operation itself. Control a Cube-Bot assigned to survive, extract valuable items, and reach extraction points to pay tax to the Taxman. Due to “CHANGE,” you’ll also receive money back — use that to purchase items from the shop that appears after every level. Work smart, extract more, survive harder.

Game Features

Extraction Gameplay

Collect and extract vital items across each level while facing escalating threats.

Tax & Change System

Pay taxes to the Taxman at extraction points — and get money back due to CHANGE mechanics.

Dynamic Shop

A new shop appears after each completed level, letting you spend wisely and upgrade your Cube-Bot.

Survival First

Enemy waves and hazards keep increasing. Only the toughest Cube-Bots last long enough to finish the Operation.
